START
    INGREDIENT = [2 SLICES OF BREAD, PEANUT BUTTER JAR, JELLY JAR]
    TOOLS = [BUTTER KNIFE, PLATE]

    OPEN PEANUT BUTTER JAR
    HOLD THE HANDLE OF THE BUTTER KNIFE
    PUT THE BLADE OF BUTTER KNIFE AND TAKE AN AMOUNT OF PEANUT BUTTER
    PUT THE PEANUT BUTTER TAKEN ON TO THE WHITE FACE OF ONE SLICE OF BREAD AND SPREAD THEM ON THE SLICE OF BREAD
    WASH AND CLEAN THE KNIFE

    OPEN JELLY JAR
    HOLD THE JELLY JAR
    POUR AN AMOUNT OF JELLY JAR ON THE WHITE FACE OF ANOTHER SLICE OF BREAD AND SPREAD THEM WITH THE BUTTER KNIFE
    WASH AND CLEAN THE KNIFE

    CLOSE THE PEANUT BUTTER JAR
    CLOSE THE JELLY JAR

    JOIN TWO SLICES OF BREAD (THE PEANUT BUTTER FACE AND THE JELLY FACE) TOGETHER
    PUT THEM ON THE PLATE
END
